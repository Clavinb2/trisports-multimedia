Willie Gormley ratified as manager for 2023 season

We are glad to announce that Willie Gormley and his management team have been ratified as managment for the 2023 club season ahead. Following a recent executive meeting, he was voted to stay on as manager, after the team's recent sucsesses, including back-to-back promotion in the league and championship finalists two years in a row.

Barry Clavin January 26, 2023 Senior team
